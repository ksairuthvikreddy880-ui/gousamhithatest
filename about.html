About Us

Learn about our journey and commitment to organic farming

Our Story

Gousamhitha was founded with a simple mission: to bring the purest, most authentic cow products to families across the country. Our journey began on a small organic farm where we witnessed the incredible benefits of traditional farming methods and the superior quality of products from happy, healthy cows.

We believe in the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda and the importance of Panchagavya - the five sacred products from the cow. Every product we offer is crafted with care, following time-honored traditions that have been passed down through generations.

Our Values

100% Organic

We never use chemicals, pesticides, or artificial hormones. Our cows graze on natural, organic pastures.

Animal Welfare

Our cows are treated with love and respect. They live in spacious, clean environments and receive the best care.

Traditional Methods

We follow ancient Vedic practices in all our processes, from milking to product preparation.

Sustainability

We practice sustainable farming that respects the environment and preserves it for future generations.

Our Process

Every product goes through rigorous quality checks. Our A2 ghee is made using the traditional bilona method, where curd is hand-churned to extract butter, which is then slowly heated to create pure ghee. This process preserves all the natural nutrients and gives our ghee its distinctive aroma and taste.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing you with products that are not just good for your health, but also good for the planet. When you choose Gousamhitha, you're supporting sustainable farming, animal welfare, and the preservation of traditional knowledge.

Thank you for being part of our journey towards a healthier, more sustainable future.
